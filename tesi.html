Nature and Technology

New collaborations between human and machine

This is a further exploration of the concept of collaboration with technology. In particular I focused on the creation of visual stories using new technologies such as artificial intelligence. The concept is then applied to the representation of the athletes’ movements and the trajectories of the ball in tennis. The final output consists in the book with the research, an abstract animation and the advertising communication for the Internazionali d’Italia tennis tournament.

Thesis Supevisors: Dionigi Mattia Gagliardi and Licia Masi
Animation Supervisor: Sara Taigher
Sound Design: Inti d'Ayala Valva
